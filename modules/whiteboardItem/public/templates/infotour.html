Move Whiteboard and Notes

You can move your notes and your whiteboard via drag and drop. This allows you to order your notes as you want. You're able to move the whiteboard and every note!


To move the whiteboard, just click on it and move it to your desire position. For notes you'll find a handler to move each one.

If you want to get rit of the note, click on the 'x' sign!


Selecting Whiteboard Items

To move more than one item on the whiteboard, you can select some, like on your desktop, and move them together. If you press ctrl you can add more items to your selection.




Delete object(s) on the Whiteboard

You can delete one or multiple objects from the whiteboard either using [DEL] or via the menu. To Delete a single object just click on the [X] at the menu next to it. To remove multiple objects or remove one (or more) objects with [DEL]] you have to select them first.
You will be prompted to confirm the delete, in this prompt you may choose if you would like to be asked again when deleting one or multiple objects.
